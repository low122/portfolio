The project is centered around the analysis of the top 1000 streamed songs on Spotify in 2023, investigating critical features that impact song rankings and performance. The objective is to comprehend patterns in song attributes, categories of artists, and their relationship with stream counts, ultimately yielding insights into the characteristics that contribute to a song's popularity. The analysis utilizes data analytics tools and methodologies to categorize and rank songs, uncover trends based on features, and provide recommendations.
